NGC 3982, also known as UGC 6918, is an intermediate spiral galaxy approximately 68 million light-years away in the constellation Ursa Major. It was discovered by William Herschel on April 14, 1789 and misclassified as planetary nebula.[4] NGC 3982 is a part of the M109 Group. At an apparent magnitude of 12.0, NGC 3982 needs a telescope to be viewed.
